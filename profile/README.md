## About Synecta

Synecta is a technology organization focused on delivering **practical, well-engineered solutions**.

We combine **young, modern engineering teams** with **experienced leadership** from multiple industries. This balance allows us to build innovative products while staying focused on clarity, simplicity, and real-world constraints.

Our approach favors capable, modern technologies — but always aims for the **simplest solution that actually solves the problem**.

Through strategic partnerships with fiscal and accounting professionals based in **San Pedro Garza García, Mexico**, Synecta can support not only the technical side of a business, but also its operational and organizational needs. This enables us to accompany clients beyond software, covering key aspects of enterprise management when required.

## Technical Philosophy

- Pragmatic over trendy
- Simplicity as a design constraint
- Maintainability and explicit architecture
- Automation-first mindset
- Technology decisions driven by context, not preference

---

## Organization Structure

Repositories in this organization generally fall into the following categories:

- **Product applications** (ERP, FE, internal platforms)
- **Shared libraries and tooling**
- **Infrastructure and DevOps**
- **Experimental or internal utilities**

Monorepos are used when they provide clear benefits in consistency, reuse, and development velocity.
